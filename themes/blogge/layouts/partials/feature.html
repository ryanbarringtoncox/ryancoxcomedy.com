ABOUT

Ryan Cox is a stand-up comedian based in Asheville, NC. As a performing songwriter and musician, Ryan started slipping jokes into his musical sets and soon became obsessed with writing the perfect punchline. In 2014 Ryan entered a Toastmasters Humorous Speech Competition and won third place in the state of North Carolina. After the competition, Ryan dove head-first into stand-up comedy and hasn't looked back.
