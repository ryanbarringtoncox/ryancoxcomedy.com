ABOUT

Ryan Cox is a touring Stand-Up Comedian based in Asheville, NC. As a lifelong songwriter and performing musician, Ryan started slipping jokes into his musical sets and became obsessed with writing the perfect punchline. In 2014 Ryan entered a Toastmasters Humorous Speech Competition and advanced to the finals, winning third place in the state of North Carolina. After the competition, Ryan was hooked and dove head-first into writing and performing jokes. Over the past decade Ryan has performed stand-up in clubs, festivals and alt rooms up and down the East Coast and worked alongside many of his favorite comics, including Reggie Watts, Pete Lee, and Jessimae Peluso.
