Ryan Cox is a stand-up comedian based in Asheville, NC. His material ranges from twisted parenting tales and crafted one-liners to the ghost of Kmart, crowd work and beyond. Ryan Cox has performed up and down the East Coast, from Comedy Key West to Bomb Shelter Comedy in NYC. He has worked in comedy clubs, alt rooms and at the North Carolina Comedy Festival. Ryan has opened for nationally touring comics like Reggie Watts, Sean Patton and Jessimae Peluso. Ryan and his wife, Emily, co-produce micro-episodes of The Xena & Hercules Podcast, available on Instagram and TikTok. Is it a real podcast or sketch comedy in disguise? (No one really knows)
